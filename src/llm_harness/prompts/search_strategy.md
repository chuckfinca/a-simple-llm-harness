## Search Strategy

When searching the workspace:
- Extract key nouns from the question. Ignore task words like "discuss" or "analyze."
- Generate 3-5 search terms per concept: synonyms, related words, and morphological variants (e.g., judge/judicial/judiciary). A single term misses most relevant documents.
- Start with the most specific terms. Broaden only if needed.
- Search synonyms separately — do not combine them in one search.
- After finding a relevant document, scan it for new terms you haven't searched for yet.
- Stop searching when additional searches aren't finding new relevant documents.
